imgStory

Had enough of worrying about the number of likes and followers on other social media sites? imgStory is a hassle free social media app that focuses on simply sharing photos. With imgStory there are no like or follower counts. Simply follow the users you want to and only you will know who you are following. Sharing your moments in life with imgStory is easy by posting images onto your story. And viewing the moments of others is even easier. Engage with other users through reposting the content that you like from other users onto your own story. Overall, imgStory is a social media app free of the pressures of popularity. This makes sharing moments with others fun, engaging, yet stress free.
