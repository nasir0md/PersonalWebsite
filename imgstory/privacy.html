imgStory Privacy Policy

Back to imgStory main page.
  1. Collected information
    • The following types of information are collected.
    • Information Provided Directly:
      • The username, display name, and password upon registration of an imgStory account.
      • The username and display name provided is shared with other users. This is for the purpose of identifying users.
      • User Content such as photos and captions that are posted to the service.
    • Analytics Information:
      • Third-party analytics tools are used to help measure traffic and usage trends for the Service. These tools collect information regarding the usage of the Service by users.
    • Metadata:
      • Metadata is data that is associated with User Content.
      • Users can have metadata associated with their content. Examples of this are having a caption with an image or the date that the content was posted.
  2. How the Information is used
    • Automatically store information so it does not have to be reentered upon each usage of the Service.
    • Provide personalized content and information in the Service.
    • Provide, improve, test, and monitor the effectiveness of our Service.
    • Develop and test new products and features.
    • Monitor metrics such as total number of visitors, traffic, and demographic patterns.
    • Diagnose or fix technology problems.
  3. Sharing of Provided Information
    • Information provided to imgStory will not be sold or rented to third parties without explicit consent.
    • Potential Parties that Information may be Shared with
      • Any information or content voluntarily posted to the Service, such as user content, is displayed to the public, as controlled by any applicable privacy settings that you set. Once you have shared user content that content may be re-shared by others through reposting or other means.
      • Removing information or content from the Service does not ensure its complete removal. Copies may exist if other users or third parties have copied or saved that information.
    • Potential for Change of Control
      • If a part or all of imgStory is sold information such as username, display name, user content and any other information collected through the Service can possibly be among the items sold or transferred. However, content will continue to be owned the user. The buyer or transferee will have to honor the commitments made in this Privacy Policy.
    • Responding to Legal Requests and Preventing Harm
      • Information can be accessed, preserved and shared in response to a legal request if there is good faith belief that the law requires doing so. This may include responding to legal requests from jurisdictions outside of the United States. Information may also be accessed, preserved and shared in order to detect, prevent and address fraud and other illegal activity. Information received about a user may be accessed, processed and retained for an extended period of time when it is the subject of a legal request or obligation, governmental investigation, or investigations concerning possible violations of the terms or policies, or otherwise to prevent harm.
  4. Storage of Information
    • Information collected through the Service may be stored and processed in any other country in which imgStory or its service providers maintain facilities.
    • imgStory, its Service Providers may transfer information that we collect about you, including personal information across borders to other countries around the world.
    • Safeguards are in place to help keep the information collected through the Service secure. However, imgStory cannot ensure the security of any information you transmit to imgStory or guarantee that information on the Service may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed.
    • Following termination or deactivation of your account, imgStory may retain information for backup or archival purposes.
  5. Children Policy
    • imgStory does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register for the Service. The Service and its content are not directed at children under the age of 13.
  6. Changes to Policy
    • imgStory may update this Privacy Policy occasionally so review it periodically. Continued use of imgStory after any update to this Privacy Policy will constitute acceptance of any such update.
